Clinical Decision Support System for the vomiting newborn

The baby in your care may have malrotation or intestinal atresia

Kindly do the following and refer the baby to a tertiary care facility.
1. Keep the baby nil per os
2. Pass a nasogastric tube (at least a size 8)
3. Start IV 10% Dextrose if the baby is within day 1 of life or IV 1/5 NS in 10% Dextrose if the baby is older than a day; at 4ml/kg/hr
4. Start broad spectrum antibiotics appropriate for age and weight
5. Call the nearest facility with a paediatric surgeon and refer the baby
You can contact the Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital Paediatric ER on 0501567104
